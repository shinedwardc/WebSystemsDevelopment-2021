GNH Tutoring

The honors program for ITWS at RPI

Tutoring

Tutoring will be in-person at the ITWS Lounge, which is located on the second floor of the Lally Building.

No form is needed to be filled, students only need to arrive on the appropriate times.

Tutoring in any course in the ITWS degree program, including, but not limited to:

  • Intro to ITWS
  • Web Systems development
  • Computer Science 1
  • Data Structures

We also help with any answering questions about career development, resumes, as well as just general questions regarding RPI.
