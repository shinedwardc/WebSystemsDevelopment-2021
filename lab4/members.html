GNH Membership

The honors program for ITWS at RPI

Membership Requirements

Students

In order to qualify for membership you must complete three semesters of study with a minimum GPA of 3.5 and must be in the top 15% of your class.

Professional Members

In order to qualify you must be named by the chapter, have made a distinctive contributuon to the IT profession, and earned one or more degree in IT or related fields with a 3.2 GPA as a undergraduate, or a 3.5 GPA as a graduate student.
