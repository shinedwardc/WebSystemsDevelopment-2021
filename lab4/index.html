About Gamma Nu Eta

Gamma Nu Eta (GNH) is the Information Technology Honor Society for college students across the U.S.

The first chapter of GNH, the Alpha Chapter, was founded at RPI in the spring of 2000. Since then, multiple other chapters have been founded, like the Beta Chapter at Illinois Institute of Technology, the Gamma Chapter at Gallaudet University and the Delta Chapter at Rutgers University.

GNH's Mission Statement

"To recognize and promote leadership and excellence in the study of Information Technology; to offer knowledge and services to our members' schools and surrounding communities; and to play an active role in the application and evolution of Information Technology within society."

GNH Activites

Gamma Nu Eta is proud to host multiple events at RPI, like regular tutoring sessions and career-related events like the Company Expose.

Tutoring

GNH hosts weekly tutoring sesstions, where we offer help with any courses in the ITWS degree program, as well as assistance with career development, resumes, and general questions about RPI.
