Article 7

Original Wording:

The Ratification of the Conventions of nine States, shall be sufficient for the Establishment of this Constitution between the States so ratifying the Same.

Summary:

This Article states that in order for the Constitution to be ratified, or approved and made into law, nine states had to agreee on it. At the time there were only 13 states, and it took about 6 months for the document to be ratified, and about 2 and a half years for all 13 states to agree.
